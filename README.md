A chronological archive of my resumes from high school graduation through my current Computer Science studies at the University of South Carolina. Each version reflects where I was at that point in time, the skills I had, the experience I had gained, and how I was presenting myself professionally.

## Versions

| Version | Year | Where I Was |
|---------|------|-------------|
| V1 | 2023 | Fresh out of high school, Home Depot associate, self-employed landscaper |
| V2 | 2024 | First year of CS, first real projects, first leadership role |
| V3 | 2024-2025 | Finding my voice, cleaner format, more technical depth |
| V4 | 2025-Present | Software engineering intern, full-stack projects in production |

## Files

Each version includes:
- `.tex` source written in LaTeX
- `.pdf` compiled output

## Compile It Yourself

```bash
pdflatex ResumeV4.tex
```

Requires [TeX Live](https://www.tug.org/texlive/) or [MiKTeX](https://miktex.org/).

## Purpose

This repo exists to show growth, not just in skills and experience, but in how I communicate my value. V1 was written by a high school graduate with a part-time job. V4 was written by a software engineer intern with real projects in production.
